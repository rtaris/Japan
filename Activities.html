Shrines/Temples

Japan is known for it's stunning temples and shrines. These temples and shrines are one of the big draws Japan has on foreigners. Fun fact temples and shrines are built to serve the Buddhist religious tradition and are characterized by a sanmon gate at the entrance. Temples and shrines have a deeper meaning than just being architectural beauties, they are used in religous gesture towards the respected reglions of Buddism and Shintoism.
Temples and shrines are historical landmarks as well. Many temples and shrines are over 1,000 years old! Most are made of wood and have to be replaced every cycle of years. The range of years very from 20 to up to 60 years dependning on the region.
While these temples and shrines are beautiful and draw a lot of attention from foreigners, it is best practice to be respectful when visiting. Temples and shrines are very spiritual places for the people of Japan. You would not want to upset them or the spirits.
